And the Tarot Cards Say...

The Devil

The Devil represents temptation, attachment, and the shadow self. Whether they are habits, fears, or unhealthy attachments.

"Breaking free from the chains of temptation is the first step toward true freedom."

Career

In your career, The Devil warns of unhealthy attachments or toxic influences. You may feel trapped by obligations, an unsatisfying job, or the pursuit of material gain at the expense of your well-being. This card encourages you to confront what is holding you back and recognize patterns that limit your growth. Breaking free from these chains can lead to greater freedom and success. A shift in perspective, where you prioritize authenticity over external validation, will pave the way for more fulfilling and meaningful career opportunities.

Love

In matters of love, The Devil suggests a focus on unhealthy patterns or attachments that may be influencing your relationship. Whether it’s codependency, jealousy, or manipulation, this card asks you to face the darker aspects of your connection. If you’re in a relationship, now is the time to break free from behaviors that are holding you both back from true intimacy. For those seeking love, be mindful of falling into relationships that repeat unhealthy patterns. Embrace self-awareness and authenticity to cultivate love that is free from fear or control.

Finance

When it comes to finances, The Devil warns of being overly attached to material success or the pursuit of wealth at any cost. You may be trapped by your desire for more, leading to impulsive or reckless financial decisions. This card urges you to examine your relationship with money and possessions, ensuring that they don’t control you. Take a step back and evaluate if your financial goals are serving your true values. By releasing unhealthy attachments, you can create a more balanced and stable financial future.
